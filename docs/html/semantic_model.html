Gold Layer — Dimension
dim_part Dimension
One row per part_id. The central dimension for all cost and revision analysis. Carries the current active revision details and a summary of total revision count and supplier coverage.
Business definition
A part is a discrete engineering component identified by a unique part_id. At any point in time, a part has exactly one current revision (where effective_to IS NULL). This dimension always reflects the current state of the part — point-in-time history lives in agg_part_revision_history.

Gold Layer — Fact
fact_bom_cost Fact
The core fact table. Grain: one row per (assembly_id, part_id, supplier_name). Contains every cost measure and flag needed for BOM cost analysis. This is the table all downstream Power BI reports and marts derive from.
Business definition
A BOM cost fact row represents a single sourcing option: a specific part used in a specific assembly, priced by a specific supplier. The fact contains both the raw cost and derived comparison metrics (is it the cheapest option? how much more expensive than cheapest?). Aggregating this table drives all assembly cost and supplier spend analysis.

Business Glossary
Definitions for all business terms used in the semantic model.
TermDefinitionUsed in
PartA discrete engineering component identified by a unique part_id in the PLM systemAll tables
RevisionA specific version of a part at a point in time. Parts have multiple revisions over their lifecycle. Only one revision is current (effective_to IS NULL) at any time.plm_revisions, agg_part_revision_history
AssemblyA product made up of multiple component parts. An assembly_id groups BOM line items.plm_bom, fact_bom_cost, agg_assembly_cost
BOM (Bill of Materials)The list of all parts and their quantities required to build a specific assemblyfact_bom_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Extended cost / Line costunit_cost × quantity. The total cost contribution of one BOM line item to the assembly cost.fact_bom_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Lowest-cost supplierThe supplier offering the cheapest unit cost for a given part. Identified by RANK()=1 partitioned by part_id.fact_bom_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Min total assembly costBest-case assembly build cost — calculated using the cheapest supplier for every part in the BOM.agg_assembly_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Avg total assembly costPlanning estimate — uses average cost across all suppliers per part, multiplied by BOM quantity.agg_assembly_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Cost premiumcost_vs_cheapest. How much more expensive a supplier's price is compared to the cheapest alternative for the same part. Zero for the cheapest supplier.fact_bom_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Supplier tierStrategic (≥30% spend share), Preferred (≥10%), Tactical (<10%). Drives procurement relationship prioritisation.agg_supplier_cost, mart_supplier_scorecard
Spend share %A supplier's lowest_cost_spend as a percentage of total platform optimistic BOM spend.agg_supplier_cost, mart_supplier_scorecard
Price freshnessHow recently a supplier's prices were updated. Current (≤30 days), Stale (≤90 days), Outdated (>90 days).mart_supplier_scorecard
Revision stageInitial Release (first-ever revision, sequence=1), Current (active today), Superseded (replaced by a newer revision).agg_part_revision_history, mart_open_revisions
Review statusOn Track (active <180 days), Review (≥180 days active), Escalate (≥365 days active). Based on PLM governance thresholds.mart_open_revisions
Cost data completenessComplete (100% of BOM parts have ERP cost), Mostly Complete (≥80%), Incomplete (<80%). Measures ERP/PLM integration coverage.agg_assembly_cost, mart_assembly_cost
Processing lagDays between a CAD file's export date (in the filename) and when it was ingested into the platform.agg_cad_activity
